About Me

From beats to bytes, Rhythm is how I connect

Hey! I'm Shravan — a musician, drummer, and tech enthusiast who loves blending creativity with technology. Music has always been a huge part of my life, from performing at festivals like the Atlanta India Festival, Access Life America, Senior Living Homes, GAMA, ATM, GATS to leading youth bands and teaching others how to play instruments like guitar and piano.

Beyond performing, I'm passionate about the technical side of music. I've been developing custom AI-powered software that helps musicians mix, record, and produce more efficiently. It's my way of combining coding and sound to make the creative process smoother and smarter.

For me, music isn't just about performing — it's about connection. It's about bringing people together, creating something meaningful, and constantly pushing boundaries through rhythm, design, and innovation.
